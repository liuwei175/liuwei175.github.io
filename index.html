About me

I'm currently working at College of Mathematics and Statistics of Chongqing University as an assistent professor. I got my Bachelor of Applied Mathematics from Xi'an Jiaotong University in 2013. Then I began to study for my doctorate. I participated in a joint PhD program between Xi'an Jiaotong University and The Hong Kong Polytechnic University. This makes me to own two PhD degrees: Doctor of Mathematics from Xi'an Jiaotong University (supervised by Prof. Zhiping Chen) and Doctor of Philosophy from The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (supervised by Prof. Xiaojun Chen). My both doctoral careers ended in Sep. 2019. In Oct. 2019, I jointed in Chongqing University located in Chongqing municipality of China to be an assistent professor in College of Mathematics and Statistics. Now I am also a postdoctoral fellow in Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS) Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science (AMSS) and the Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) Joint Laboratory of Applied Mathematics which is located in Hong Kong since 12 Mar. 2020.
